Lilly Clark

Network Software Engineer @ Starlink

I'm a software engineer at SpaceX, where I am responsible for delivering performant satellite software to provide high throughput, low-latency global coverage for fast-movers including our commercial airline customers, Falcon rockets, and Starship.
Prior to SpaceX, I attended graduate school at the University of Southern California where I received my PhD in Electrical Engineering. My work in the Autonomous Networks Research Group focused on connectivity modeling, collaborative localization, and communication-aware autonomy for multi-robot teams exploring harsh unknown environments. I graduated from MIT with BS in Aerospace Engineering in 2018.
